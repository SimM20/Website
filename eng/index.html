INGENIERÍA ALPA

ALPA is a privately-owned family company founded in August 1988 by Horacio and Alfredo Zubiri, established to participate in Argentina's privatization processes.

In 1990, ALPA joined the Argentine oil industry as a producer and operator in the Golfo San Jorge and Neuquén basins.

Since its inception, and for the development of up-stream projects, it has formed partnerships with various local and international companies.
