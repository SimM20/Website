ENVIROMENT

ALPA undertakes the commitment to carry out its activities with the fundamental priorities of ensuring the safety of operations, the health of the individuals involved, and the preservation of the environment in which it operates.

Our Principles

Comply with applicable laws and regulations on safety, health, and the environment.

Operate by making rational use of energy and natural resources.

All hazards and environmental impacts resulting from activities and facilities must be identified, assessed, and managed.

Safe, healthy, and environmentally friendly working conditions must be provided to employees, contractors, and the community, thereby preventing occupational diseases, accidents, and incidents.

Promote that contractors and suppliers have safety, health, and environmental performance that is consistent with this policy.

Implement emergency devices and procedures to respond quickly and effectively to accidents and incidents, with the goal of minimizing their consequences.

Ensure the training of all permanent and contracted personnel in safety, health, and environmental matters to achieve awareness and competence for the execution of their activities.

Ensure positive progress in safety, health, and environmental performance through periodic evaluations and identifying areas for improvement.

Accidents and incidents will be documented and investigated in order to prevent their recurrence and consequences.

Each person is responsible for their own safety, as well as for collaborating in the safety of their colleagues and ensuring proper environmental protection.
