OUR STORY

About us

ALPA is a privately-owned family company founded in August 1988 by Horacio and Alfredo Zubiri, established to participate in the privatization processes in Argentina.

Since 1990, ALPA has participated in the Argentine oil industry as a producer and operator in the Golfo San Jorge and Neuquén basins.

Since its inception, and for the development of upstream projects, it has formed partnerships with various local and international companies.


"ALPA's vision is to develop as an exploration, production, and operations company in the Argentine oil industry."

Ingeniería Alpa S.A. announces the appointment of Mr. Alberto Armando Boix to its Board of Directors as a Full Director.
Mr. Alberto Armando Boix is an actuary from the University of Buenos Aires, holds an MBA with a specialization in Finance and International Business from the University of Chicago, and has over 30 years of experience in executive positions and as a director in investment banks and industries.
We wish Alberto the best of luck and trust that his presence on the Board will bring a strategic vision for the company's growth.
